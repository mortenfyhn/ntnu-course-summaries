\paragraph{Odd function}  $f(x) = - f(-x)$ Odd functions reduce to
Fourier sine series.

If $f(x)$ i given for $0 \leq x \leq L$ only, $f(x)$ has two half
range expansions of period $2L$, namely, a cosine and sine series.
